Welcome To Athena

We're a nonprofit dedicated to helping families get access to free books and toys, free of charge.

About Us

We've been mission-focused on giving the gifts of toys and free education since 2018. We collect books, toys, resources, and distribute them to families in need. Our strategic partnerships with community-focused: with libraries, shelters, community centers, parents, and donors. We aim to increase literacy countrywide, one family at a time.
