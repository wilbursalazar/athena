## Overview
Welcome to athena, the nonprofit dedicated to giving toys and education free of charge. Our website is delivered in a single page HTML site designed to inform you about what we do, who we partner with, and a contact option for you.

## Functionality / How To Use

Click the navigation bar to navigate the site or simply scroll down. Type your contact information into our contact section.

## Tech Stack

HTML, CSS, Git and GitHub, GitHub Pages, and English.

## Our Mission
We aim to give the gift of fun and education. To this end, we partner with libraries, hospitals, churches, and other nonprofits to collect books and toys for children in need.
